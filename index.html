What is Ragnar?

Ragnar is a video game compatible on Windows, Mac, Linux, and Android. The player has just arrived in England, and is being ambushed by English bowmen. Avoid the bowmen's arrows to gain honor until you get enough to reach Valhalla. Score is based off of time in seconds that you survive.

Who is Ragnar?

Ragnar Lothbrok is a Viking Legend and the scrouge of England. He led many raids and expeditions to England.
